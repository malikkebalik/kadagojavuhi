Pamela Veasey is an American writer, producer, and director who has a net worth of $8 million. Born in Texas on May 25, 1962, Veasey grew up in Pullman, Washington. She attended the University of Southern California and received degrees in Journalism and Political Science. She has been writing actively since 1985 and got her start with the series Gimme a Break! where she worked as a receptionist and eventually wrote six episodes. Following that, she wrote an episode of What's Happening Now!!
