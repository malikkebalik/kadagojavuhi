Not ZEE5, but this OTT platform to premiere Boys Hostel soon

August 04, 2024
blog

Kannada super hit Hostel Hudugaru Bekagiddare has been dubbed into Telugu as Boys Hostel and made its theatrical debut on August 26, 2023. The Kannada version was already available for streaming on ZEE5. Now, there’s an interesting update regarding Boys Hostel’s OTT release.

Contrary to expectations, the digital streaming rights for the movie were secured by the popular Telugu channel ETV’s streaming service, ETV Win. They have made it certain that the movie will be premiered very soon. According to reliable sources, the movie is scheduled to premiere on November 3, 2023, on ETV Win.

Directed by debutant Nithin Krishnamurthy, the film features actors Prajwal BP, Manjunath Nayaka, Rakesh Rajkumar, Srivatsa, and Tejas Jayanna Urs in significant roles. Tharun Bhascker and Rashmi are also part of this movie.

Presented by Rakshit Shetty under Paramvah Pictures, the film boasts a musical score composed by Ajaneesh Loknath. This cinematic creation is a collaborative effort between Gulmohur Films and Varrun Studios. Stay tuned for more updates on OTT releases.
