Early Life

James Cameron, originally James Francis Cameron, was born on August the 16th, 1954, in Kapuskasing, Ontario, Canada. His father, Philip Cameron, was an electrical engineer, and his mother, Shirley, was an artist and a nurse.

His family moved to Brea, California, in the states. He was 17 years of age at that time. He went to Sonora High School and later attended Brea Olinda High School.

As per his classmates, Cameron was not the kind of guy who loved sports; instead, he liked building stuff.

After completing high school, James attended a community college named Fullerton College in 1973 to study physics. But later switched back to English and dropped out of the college at the end of 1974.

He did odd jobs such as janitor, truck driver but wrote in his free time. During this particular time, he developed a passion for film technology, special effects.

He also learned about them by reading their student’s work. Eventually, he left his job as a truck driver and entered the film industry.
