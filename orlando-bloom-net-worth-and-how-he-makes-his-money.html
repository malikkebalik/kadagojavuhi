Orlando Bloom Net Worth and How He Makes His Money

Here’s Orlando Bloom’s net worth and how he makes his money.

Early years and rise to fame

Orlando Bloom made his acting debut in 1994 in the television series Casualty. He played the role of Noel Harrison for three episodes. After that, he appeared in the 1997 movie Wilde. Bloom got his big break in 2001 when he appeared in the movie The Lord of the Rings: Fellowship of the Ring. That same year, he starred in the film Black Hawk Down. Other notable movies of his include The Lord of the Rings: The Two Towers (2002), Pirates of the Caribbean: The Curse of the Black Pearl (2003), and Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Man’s Chest (2006).

Awards

In 2002, Bloom won an MTV Movie Award for best breakthrough male performance for The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring. Two years later, he won a Screen Actors Guild Award for outstanding performance by a cast in a motion picture for The Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King. One of the highlights of Bloom’s career was when he was awarded a star on The Hollywood Walk of Fame in 2014.

Bloom says he struggled in his career

After the success of The Lord of the Rings and Pirates of the Caribbean franchises, Bloom said he found himself struggling to regain popularity. In an interview with Elle, the actor says his career thrived during his twenties, but he began to struggle during his thirties. He said he had difficulty dealing with the fact he was no longer one of the most popular actors in the world:

I had so much success in my twenties, most of my thirties was [spent] figuring out which way was up. I love what I do. It came from that connection kids felt with [The Lord of the Rings’] Legolas, or [Pirates of the Caribbean’s] Will Turner. I’m looking for that thing again… You’re no longer number one in the world. You look at everything around you and ask, ‘What is going on?’

Personal life

Bloom has been romantically linked to quite a few celebrity women. Some of the stars he has been linked to include Condola Rashad, Helena Christensen, Jennifer Aniston, Kate Bosworth, Kirsten Dunst, Lindsay Lohan, Liv Tyler, Miranda Kerr, Penelope Cruz, Naomie Harris, and Selena Gomez. Bloom became engaged to singer Katy Perry in February 2019. Bloom was married to model Miranda Kerr from 2010 to 2013. They have one son together named Flynn.

How Orlando Bloom makes his money

Bloom also earns money as a producer. Some of the projects he has produced include Haven (2004), The Good Doctor (2011), and The Greasy Hands Preachers (2014). Furthermore, he makes money from product endorsements. One of his collaborations was an advertising campaign with British Airways.

Orlando Bloom’s net worth

Orlando Bloom has an estimated net worth of $35 million.
