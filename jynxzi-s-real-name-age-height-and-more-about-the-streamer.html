How would one describe Jynxzi? Simple, he is the popular Twitch streamer who came out of nowhere to dethrone the platform’s biggest streamers with a huge jump in active subscribers that boosted him well ahead of the competition.

But, who is Jynxzi, and what else do we know about the new king of Twitch?

Jynxzi’s age and height

The famous Twitch streamer was born on Sept. 26, 2001, making him 22 years old at the time of writing. He is 5 ft 10, which the streamer confirmed himself on camera after fans claimed that he was closer to 5 ft 2.

In the video, Jynxzi shows his height as well as his weight (which is 162.9 pounds, if you were curious). While the latter is obviously subject to fluctuations, his height probably hasn’t changed.

What else do we know about Jynxzi?

Not a great deal is known about Jynxzi’s private life, but we know his real name is Nicholas Stewart and that he was born in Washington. Back in 2021, he was asked if he had a girlfriend to which he responded, “I don’t have a girlfriend bro. I play Siege all day.” Thus, by all accounts, it seems like Jynxzi is currently single. 

Jynxzi has a pretty impressive estimated net worth of about $725,000, most of which comes from Twitch, and that number is probably only set to grow considering he is the most subscribed streamer on the platform.

Before Twitch, Jynxzi claims he was heavily involved in esports for a couple of years, playing Clash Royale professionally as a teen.

Jynxzi’s rise to fame

Jynxzi rose to popularity on Twitch mostly for playing Rainbow Six Siege. He started in 2019 by making comedic and competitive gaming videos of multiplayer games. He eventually became a Twitch partner as well as expanded into YouTube before really starting to grow his audience in 2020.

Success came to Jynxzi pretty fast, mostly thanks to his unique content and personality, it’s also been helped by the fact that he’s managed to avoid controversy, something which seems to always happen with big streamers. Jynxzi has remained drama-free and continues to create quality content for his fans, so it makes sense that he would be one of the most popular content creators on the site.

He overtook the likes of XQC and Kai Cenat (the previous king of Twitch) as the streamer with the most active subscribers back in April, a development he became aware of while streaming. Since then, he’s continued to grow at a rapid pace and now he’s got a pretty sizeable lead with nearly 130,000, putting him almost 50,000 ahead of his closest competition.

With numbers like that, it’s safe to say Jynxzi will probably stay the number one Twitch streamer for quite some time.
