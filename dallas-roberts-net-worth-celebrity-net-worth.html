What is Dallas Roberts' Net Worth?

Dallas Roberts is an American actor who has a net worth of $2 million. Dallas Roberts was born in Houston, Texas in May 1970. From 2006 to 2009 he starred as Angus Partridge on the television series The L Word. Roberts starred as Miles Fielder on the TV series Rubicon in 2010. From 2012 to 2013 he starred as Milton Mamet on the series The Walking Dead. Dallas Roberts starred as Eliot Delson on the television series Unforgettable from 2013 to 2015.

He has also had recurring roles on the TV series Law & Order: Special Victims Unit as Greg Yates and American Crime as Carson Hesby. Dallas Roberts has appeared in several films including Walk the Line, Flicka, 3:10 to Yuma, Dallas Buyers Club, and more.
