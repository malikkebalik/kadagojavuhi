Eden Hazard Net Worth and salary: Eden Hazard is a Belgian soccer player who has a net worth of $100 million. Eden Hazard was born on January 7, 1991 in La Louviere, Belgium. After playing on several Belgian under-21 squads, he finally joined the professional team in 2008, making 45 appearances and scoring six goals through May 2014.

In the Premier League, he played for Lille from 2007-12, scoring 50 goals in 192 appearances for the club. He joined Chelsea in 2012, scoring 30 goals in two seasons (111 appearances). Hazard has won Ligue 1 and the Coupe de France in 2010-11 with Lille, and the UEFA Europa League in 2012-13 with Chelsea. Hazard has earned a number of individual honors, as well: he was twice the UNFP Ligue 1 Young Player of the Year (2008-09 and again in 2009-10), twice the UNFP Ligue 1 Player of the Year (2010-11, 2011-12), and made the UNFMP Ligue 1 Team of the Year in three consecutive seasons from 2009-2012.
