Is the Dyson Supersonic worth it? 

Yes, I can tell you firsthand that this really is the best hair dryer. Before I ever heard of the Supersonic, I didn't even know a hair dryer could be that expensive. Then, when I finally invested in one for myself, it genuinely delivered everything I could ask for in a hair dryer — which is a lot, because I have thick, curly hair.

The Dyson hair dryer dries my hair quickly without causing it to fry against a hot air vent. I get the most use out of its medium heat setting and curl-diffusing attachment, but regardless of what routine suits your hair type best, the Supersonic will generate negative ions that work to break up water molecules and seal the hair's cuticle in order to provide a smooth, frizz-free finish.

It helps that the Supersonic is so lightweight, because it takes much less effort to lift above my head, and it's easy to pack in a weekend bag. I also appreciate that it's remarkably quiet, as its tiny motor is inaudible to the human ear, and the only sound it makes is from its airflow.
